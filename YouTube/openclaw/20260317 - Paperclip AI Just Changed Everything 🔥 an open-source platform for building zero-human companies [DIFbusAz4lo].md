## Paperclip: an open-source agent orchestration that runs “zero-human” companies—and your life

This video demos Paperclip, an open-source orchestration platform that lets you create organizations and AI agents (a CEO agent, specialists, project workspaces) to accept goals, decompose them into issues, spin up new agents, and execute tasks autonomously in a local working folder and browser UI. The presenter runs a live example: install, create an organization, assign a CEO agent a goal like “grow revenue by 150%,” have that CEO create an accounting agent, and track issues and priorities; the repo is active with about 26,000 stars and a recent release, and the creator says they spent 100+ hours building personal “skills” that feed agents. *open-source orchestration for zero human companies*

The core pitch is practical: map areas of your life (health, taxes, wealth, career, travel) to projects, give clear goals, and let agents fetch documents, analyze calendars/records, recommend actions, and hire or configure subagents as needed. The presenter shows a skills folder structure, local workspaces per project, low-cost execution in his demo (he reports $2 spent so far), and argues modest improvements across many domains compound into large gains; he emphasizes using an orchestrator to move beyond simple email summarization toward continuous life and business optimization. *you could be the CEO of your own life.*
