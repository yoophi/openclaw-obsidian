## 25 AI agents managed from a single dashboard

The creator demonstrates a custom operations dashboard that runs 25 autonomous agents around the clock under a hierarchical org chart: a human CEO for vision, a central “COO” brain that handles research and delegation, and three department leads for tech, marketing, and revenue. The system routes work across a model fleet (a primary heavy-lifter, fallbacks, and lightweight models for community tasks), pairs stronger models for research with smaller models for final output, and tracks sessions, token use, cron jobs, overnight logs, and estimated costs in a unified task manager. *"I have 25 agents running 24/7 autonomously around the clock."*

Key workflows and capabilities shown: automated standups where agents converse, create action items, and produce transcripts plus a voice notification; per-workspace identities and persistent memory so specific bots (one with its own “heartbeat”) remember conversations and check in with people; real-time documentation that updates as the ops dashboard changes; and modular divisions (backend/security, frontend/DevOps, QA, content, community, product/growth) with different model assignments. *"Muddy is always available to me. He never cues prompts. He always delegates."*
