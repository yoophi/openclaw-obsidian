## CMUX Just Changed Everything for Developers using Agentic CLIs

This demo shows a Mac-native terminal multiplexer (referred to in the clip as CMox/CMOX/CMUX) that organizes many agentic CLIs into channel-like tabs so you can run multiple agents (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, etc.) concurrently without juggling windows. The presenter demonstrates color-coded channels, per-channel tabs, a built-in browser for quick tests, job notifications when long tasks finish, and the ability to SSH to a remote Mac Mini so background processing can run off your laptop.

He also demos a small utility called "AI defe"/"AI dev" with a single command workflow (dev deploy) that copies code, symlinks and secrets to the Mac Mini so agent pipelines and automations pick up the latest files; sessions can be closed and reopened, though he notes session settings were lost after quitting in his test. The overall verdict: this multiplexer replaces a messy tmux-based setup and dramatically speeds up agentic, multi-agent terminal workflows. *"hands down one of the most most exciting tools I have found from an agentic perspective."* *"It's just orders of magnitude better than that."*
